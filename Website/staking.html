The ClassicDAO crowdsale is designed to be the simplest yet most efficient way to run a fair distribution of an ERC20 token. For more information on exactly how this crowdsale operates, please refer to our documentation page.

If you are unsure as to the steps that must be completed in order to participate, check out our participation guide page or our video based tutorials.

To gather further insight before participating, feel free to take a look at our main website, the whitepaper or any of our social medias.

Lastly, if you have any questions, concerns or require some help, do not hesitate to message us on twitter, or message one of the admins on our discord.

Thank you for participating in what we are building, we couldn’t do it without you!

Please Connect your Web3 Wallet such as Metamask, TrustWallet or Brave Browser wallet in order to participate in the Crowdsale.
